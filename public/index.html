Process

From Start to Finish

  • Idea

    The order process generally takes about five business days from when you first place your order to the order's completion. Start by coming in to see Johnny (or Keisha) to place your order.Explain to us your vision...

  • Art Work

    The art department then creates your artwork and sends you a digital proof...

  • Approval

    After we receive your approval the artwork is then put on screen awaiting production.

  • Press

    Our skilled printers then setup the screen on one of our five state of the art presses. And production of your job begins.

  • Cure

    After the shirt is printed it is sent down a conveyor belt where it is cured by a high temperature dryer unit.

  • Examination

    Your shirts are then examined for discrepancies, counted, and arranged by size.

  • Ship

    Finally, the "Happy Ending"... We neatly pack and package your order for pick-up or shipping.

How can I place my order?
There are a few ways. One is you can come into the store and place your order. Secondly, via the fax or phone. Thirdly, through our website or email address. We try to accommodate the customer and make it most convenient for them.

What is the turn around time for an order?
Usually about 5 business days. Regardless of quantity.

What file formats do you accept?
We accept vector artwork in .ai or .eps form. If artwork is simple we will accept jpeg's, tiff's and photoshop's psd format.

What are your t shirt sizes?
Youth shirts XS(2-4), S(6-8), M(10-12), L (14-16) Adult shirts small, medium, large, x-large, xx-large, xxx-large (White, black, red, navy, available thru 5x. Royal blue available thru 4x.)

Is there an art charge?
Our esteemed designers can create or recreate anything. Only basic artwork is free. Charges vary with time and complication.

Can I provide my own artwork?
Absolutely! Submit your vector artwork in eps form, or photoshop psd format. Submitted jpeg's or pdf's might require an art charge because we will have to reproduce them to give you the best quality prints.

Are rush orders available?
Yes for a small charge shirts can be printed and shipped within 24 hours.
